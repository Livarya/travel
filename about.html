Our Story

Crafting Luxury Travel Experiences Since 2010

Livarya Travel was founded with a simple yet powerful vision: to redefine luxury travel by creating personalized, immersive experiences that capture the essence of each destination. What began as a boutique agency in Jakarta has grown into Indonesia's premier luxury travel company, serving clients from around the world.

Our team of experienced travel designers works closely with a network of local partners and luxury properties to craft journeys that go beyond the ordinary. We believe that true luxury is found in the details—the perfect view from your suite, the private guide who reveals hidden gems, or the surprise celebration arranged without you asking.

Our Core Values

The principles that guide our approach to luxury travel and service excellence

Excellence

We strive for excellence in every detail, from the moment you begin planning until your return home. No request is too small and no dream too big for our team to fulfill.

Sustainability

We are committed to responsible travel that respects local cultures and preserves natural environments for future generations to enjoy. Luxury and sustainability can coexist beautifully.

Innovation

We continuously explore new destinations, experiences, and technologies to enhance your journey and provide access to extraordinary moments that others can't offer.

Relationships

We value long-term relationships with our clients, partners, and communities. Many of our clients have been traveling with us for years, returning for new adventures.
